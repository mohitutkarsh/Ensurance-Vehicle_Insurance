Two-Wheeler Vehicle Insurance Registration

Documents Required For Two Wheeler new Insurance or old Insurance Renewal :-

  • -> Proof of identity documents (driving license/passport/government issued photo ID/PAN card/Aadhaar card).
  • -> Policyholder’s driving license information.
  • -> Old insurance policy number.
  • -> Two wheeler registration certificate number and registration number.
  • -> Net/banking details/credit card/debit card details (for online payments).

Documents required for Insurance claim :-

  • -> Policy Number of the insurance
  • -> Policyholder’s information such as an address, occupation, name, gender, date of birth.
  • -> License of the person due to whom the damage to the vehicle has been done
